1. Introduction & Background 1. Pendahuluan & Latar Belakang

The Decentralized Finance (DeFi) landscape has grown exponentially, yet mass adoption remains severely hindered by two primary bottlenecks: fragmented liquidity and an overly complex User Experience (UX). Everyday users frequently struggle with seed phrases, gas fee management, and highly inefficient yield farming optimization processes.

Zoniq Finance (ZoniqFi) emerges as a cutting-edge solution built on the Base L2 ecosystem. By leveraging Account Abstraction (ERC-4337) and a Vote-Escrowed (ve) governance model, ZoniqFi establishes a unified DeFi gateway that offers seamless transactions, highly efficient liquidity routing, and sustainable Real Yield distribution to token holders.

Lanskap Decentralized Finance (DeFi) telah berkembang pesat, namun adopsi massal masih terhambat oleh dua masalah utama: fragmentasi likuiditas dan pengalaman pengguna (User Experience/UX) yang terlalu rumit. Pengguna awam sering kali kesulitan menghadapi seed phrases, pengelolaan gas fee, dan optimalisasi hasil (yield farming) yang tidak efisien.

Zoniq Finance (ZoniqFi) hadir sebagai solusi mutakhir di ekosistem Base L2. Dengan memanfaatkan arsitektur Account Abstraction (ERC-4337) dan model tata kelola Vote-Escrowed (ve), ZoniqFi menciptakan platform DeFi satu pintu yang menawarkan transaksi tanpa hambatan, likuiditas yang efisien, dan bagi hasil riil (Real Yield) bagi para pemegang token.

2. Core Features & Product Architecture 2. Fitur Utama & Arsitektur Produk

2.1 Next-Gen Smart Account (Account Abstraction)

2.1 Next-Gen Smart Account (Account Abstraction)

ZoniqFi eliminates conventional Web3 onboarding friction through the native integration of Smart Accounts.

  • Social Logins & Passkeys: Users can create non-custodial wallets in seconds using Google, Apple ID, or biometric Passkeys.
  • Gasless Transactions: A dedicated paymaster mechanism allows developers or protocols to sponsor transaction fees, or enables users to settle gas fees using stablecoins such as USDC.
  • Batched Transactions: Multi-step executions (e.g., Token Approval + Vault Deposit) are condensed into a single-click operation.

ZoniqFi menghilangkan hambatan masuk Web3 konvensional melalui integrasi Smart Account.

  • Akses Sosial & Passkey: Pengguna dapat membuat dompet non-kustodian dalam hitungan detik menggunakan Google, Apple ID, atau Passkey biometrik.
  • Gasless Transactions: Mekanisme paymaster memungkinkan pengembang atau protokol mensponsori biaya transaksi (gas fee), atau mengizinkan pengguna membayar gas menggunakan token stabil seperti USDC.
  • Batched Transactions: Eksekusi multi-langkah (seperti Approve + Deposit ke dalam Vault) dapat dilakukan hanya dalam satu kali klik.

2.2 Automated Compounding Vaults (Yield Optimizer)

2.2 Automated Compounding Vaults (Yield Optimizer)

ZoniqFi Vaults operate as automated asset managers designed to maximize yields for Liquidity Providers (LPs).

  • Auto-Compounding: The protocol periodically harvests reward incentives, swaps them back into the underlying primary assets, and automatically reinvests them to trigger a powerful compounding interest effect.
  • Efficiency on Base: Capitalizing on the ultra-low transaction costs of the Base L2 network, compounding cycles are executed at higher frequencies to deliver optimized APYs.

ZoniqFi Vaults bertindak sebagai manajer investasi otomatis yang memaksimalkan pendapatan penyedia likuiditas (LP).

  • Auto-Compounding: Protokol secara berkala memanen (harvest) hadiah insentif, menukarnya kembali menjadi aset utama, dan menginvestasikannya ulang secara otomatis untuk memicu efek bunga berbunga (compound interest).
  • Efficiency on Base: Berkat biaya transaksi yang sangat murah di jaringan Base L2, proses reinvestasi dapat dilakukan lebih sering demi meraih APY yang optimal.

2.3 Concentrated Liquidity (DEX Swap)

2.3 Sentralisasi Likuiditas (DEX Swap)

ZoniqFi integrates a built-in DEX aggregator featuring intelligent smart routing. This ensures minimal slippage and the most competitive transaction rates for users swapping digital assets.

ZoniqFi mengintegrasikan agregator DEX internal dengan rute cerdas (smart routing) untuk memastikan slippage minimal dan biaya transaksi termurah bagi pengguna yang ingin menukar aset digital mereka.

3.2 Vote-Escrowed Mechanism (veZNF)

3.2 Mekanisme Vote-Escrowed (veZNF)

Users can lock their $ZNF tokens for a specified duration ranging from 1 week up to a maximum of 4 years. The longer the tokens are locked, the greater the voting power and share of protocol revenue accrued.

Pengguna dapat mengunci token $ZNF mereka untuk jangka waktu tertentu (dari 1 minggu hingga maksimal 4 tahun). Semakin lama token dikunci, semakin besar hak suara (Voting Power) dan hak bagi hasil yang diperoleh.

Voting Power = Amount of ZNF × (Lock Duration / 4 Years)

3.3 Incentive Distribution (Gauge Voting)

3.3 Distribusi Insentif (Gauge Voting)

Every week (per Epoch), veZNF holders can cast votes on the Gauge Allocation Table. This process dynamically dictates the distribution percentage of daily $ZNF$ token emissions across the available liquidity pools and vaults. This setup sparks a healthy competition among pools to attract deep capital, driving the Liquidity Flywheel.

Setiap minggu (per Epoch), para pemegang veZNF dapat memberikan suara (voting) pada Gauge Allocation Table. Proses ini menentukan ke kolam likuiditas (Pool/Vault) mana emisi harian token $ZNF$ baru akan dialokasikan. Mekanisme ini memicu persaingan sehat antar-pool untuk menarik likuiditas terbesar (Liquidity Flywheel).

3.4 Real Yield (Protocol Revenue Share)

3.4 Real Yield (Pendapatan Riil Protokol)

ZoniqFi distributes Real Yield rather than relying solely on token inflation. 60% of all swap fees and Vault performance fees are collected by the protocol in blue-chip assets (USDC/WETH) and distributed weekly directly to veZNF stakers.

ZoniqFi tidak hanya membagikan inflasi token, melainkan Real Yield. Sebesar 60% dari seluruh biaya penukaran (Swap Fees) dan biaya performa Vault dikumpulkan oleh protokol dalam bentuk pecahan USDC/WETH, kemudian didistribusikan setiap minggu secara langsung kepada para pemegang token veZNF.
